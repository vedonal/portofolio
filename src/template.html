Projects

DFW Best Transportation

DFW Transportation is a website that allows users to book a ride. There are two types of services offered for users that were requested by the client, charter and hourly. Upon selecting one of the services the user can fill in the information by selecting a date and time, a pickup location, and a drop-off location and then it calculates the price based on the distance (mile x price/mile) or hour of the ride which works by integrating google maps API. The user then can see a summary of the ride and put the billing information. It also makes it able to add a gratuity for the driver that is added to the total price. PayPal, Credit Card, Apple Pay and Google Pay are available payment methods for users to finish their booking ride.
